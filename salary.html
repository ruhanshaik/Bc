Understanding the Salary Data

Salary data is presented in Lakhs Per Annum (LPA) and is based on market averages for India in . Actual pay varies greatly based on company type (MNC vs. Startup), location (Bangalore/Mumbai/Delhi), and specialized skills.

Freshers (0-2 Yrs)

Entry-level roles, including initial training or internship conversion. Figures often reflect lower-tier service companies.

Mid-Experience (3-7 Yrs)

Roles requiring independent contribution, team lead potential, and deep technical or functional knowledge.

Senior/Expert (8+ Yrs)

Leadership, Architect, or Principal level roles responsible for strategy and mentoring. High-end salaries include large bonuses/ESOPs.
