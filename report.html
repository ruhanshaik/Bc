Report a Problem or Suspicious Activity

Your vigilance is crucial in helping us maintain a safe and trusted job platform for everyone in the Bangalore Connect community. We strictly review all reported issues to ensure our job listings remain genuine, verified, and free from misleading or fraudulent activities.

1. Types of Issues to Report

If you notice anything suspicious, incorrect, or not working properly on our website, please report it to us. This helps us keep Bangalore Connect safe and reliable for all job seekers.

  • Fake or Scam Listings: Job posts asking for money, paid interviews, paid training, or unnecessary personal details.
  • Expired Jobs: Jobs that are no longer active, outdated, or lead to broken pages instead of a valid application link.
  • Technical Problems: Broken links, loading issues, or any errors you face while using the Bangalore Connect website.
  • Wrong Information: Incorrect company names, wrong job location, wrong salary details, or misleading contact information.

2. How to Submit Your Report

If you find anything that looks wrong, unsafe, or suspicious on our website, please report it to us as soon as possible. Your feedback helps us keep Bangalore Connect safe, clean, and trustworthy for all job seekers.

You can send your report directly to our support team by email. When you contact us, please include the details below so we can quickly understand the issue and fix it without delay.

  • Job Title: The exact job title as shown on the website.
  • Company Name: The company name mentioned in the listing.
  • Job Link: The full URL of the job post so our team can check it instantly.
  • Description of the Issue: Tell us what problem you faced. For example: “The link is not opening,” “They are asking for money,” “Information is wrong,” or “This job looks fake.”
  • Your Device or Browser (optional): This helps us understand if the issue is related to a specific device or browser.

📧 Report Email: bangalore.connect1@gmail.com

We appreciate every report we receive, and each one helps make the platform safer for everyone. Your effort supports thousands of job seekers who rely on Bangalore Connect for genuine and verified job updates.

3. Our Commitment and Review Process

We take every report seriously. Once we receive your message, our moderation team checks the issue quickly to make sure the platform stays safe for all users.

Here is how our review process works:

  • Fast Review: Most reports are checked within 12 business hours.
  • Immediate Action: If we confirm that a job post is fake, misleading, or unsafe, we remove it from the website right away.
  • Your Privacy: Your identity is always kept private. We never share your name or contact details with the company or any third party.

Thank you for helping us keep Bangalore Connect safe, honest, and useful for every job seeker. Your support makes a big difference.
