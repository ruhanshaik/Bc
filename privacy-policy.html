Privacy Policy

At Bangalore Connect, your privacy truly matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains, in simple words, what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe. When you use the Bangalore Connect website, apply for jobs, or explore our services, you trust us — and we take that responsibility seriously.

This Privacy Policy applies to everything we offer, including all pages, tools, and features on the Bangalore Connect platform. Our main aim is to be clear and honest about how your data is handled, so you can use our website with full confidence.

We only collect the information needed to improve your experience, show relevant job updates, and make the platform work smoothly. We do not sell or misuse your personal data — your information stays protected and secure.

1. Introduction

At Bangalore Connect, we respect your privacy and care about your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains, in simple words, what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe. We request all users to read this policy so you clearly understand how your information is handled on our website.

2. Personal Information

We may collect basic personal details like your name, email address, phone number, or any information you share with us through forms, job alerts, or contact pages. We use this information to send job updates, reply to your messages, and provide better support whenever you need it.

3. Non-Personal Information

When you visit our website, some information is collected automatically. This includes your IP address, device type, browser type, pages you visit, and how long you stay on the site. This data helps us understand what users like, improve website speed, and make the overall experience better.

4. How We Use Collected Information

  • To run and improve our website and services.
  • To send job alerts, emails, and updates through email or WhatsApp.
  • To make our website easier to use and more helpful for job seekers.
  • To keep our platform safe and monitor unwanted activities.
  • To respond to your questions and provide customer support.

5. Advertising and Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to show ads on our website. Google uses a DART cookie to display ads based on your visits to our site and other websites. These ads help keep our platform free for users. If you do not want personalized ads, you can opt out by visiting the Google Ads Privacy Policy or Network Advertising Initiative.

6. Data Protection and Security

We follow safe and secure methods to store and protect your information. Only authorized team members can access your data, and they must keep it private. We do not share your personal information with anyone unless required for essential services.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience, understand traffic, and show relevant content. Cookies do not store your passwords or sensitive details. If you want, you can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some parts of the website may not work properly.

8. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for how those websites collect or use your data. We suggest you read their privacy policies before sharing personal information on those sites.

9. Your Acceptance of These Terms

By using our website, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please avoid using the site. If we update the policy, your continued use of the website means you accept the new changes.
